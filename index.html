About me

Hello there! My name is Akriti and I am a telecommunications engineer by profession. I am also a wife, a daughter, a sister , and an aspiring ally.

On the work front, a large part of my job includes testing the performance of telecom software solutions on carrier grade servers. These software solutions are then provided to various tier 1 telecom providers. I also engage with field teams and Solutions engineers on reproducing and fixing customer issues. Currently the most exciting part of my job is working on container-based cloud native technology and Kubernetes.

On my days off, I like to travel with my husband. We are both nature lovers, and most of our trips end up becoming national park adventures all over America. I am also a brand new plant parent whose biggest acheivement is saving a dying Monstera. Please explore the website to find out more about me.
